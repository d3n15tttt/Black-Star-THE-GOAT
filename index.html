Black☆Star is an assassin, shadow weapon meister, and the last survivor of the infamous Star Clan taken in the DWMA and raised by Sid Barrett. In the DWMA, his weapon partner is Tsubaki Nakatsukasa, he is one of the top students in the academy as well as a former member of Spartoi.
